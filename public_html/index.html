Who We Are

The AFSHu

Welcome to the Association of Filipino Students in Hungary. Discover the vibrant community of the Association of Filipino Students in Hungary. Connect with fellow students, celebrate our culture, and foster meaningful friendships. We are more than just an organization - we are a family away from home. Experience the warmth, support, and camaraderie that defines our community. Whether you're a new student looking to meet friends or a seasoned member seeking new adventures, our doors are always open. Join us in creating lasting memories, making a difference, and celebrating the Filipino spirit.
